Update History
 
The update history shows the summary of changes in Frame Grabber SDK with different versions.
Version

Description

V2.3.0 2023/7

Newly Added:

  • Synchronized SDK functions of Linux and Windows system, and updated Linux version No. to 2.3.0 together with the Windows version.
  • Added support for controlling CoaXPress/XoFLink/Camera Link/GigE Vision frame grabbers in multiple processes.
  • Added APIs for initializing SDK resources and releasing SDK resources: MV_FG_Initialize(), MV_FG_Finalize()
  • Added and updated C++ sample programs.

Optimized:

  • Optimized frame grabber events' performance and stability.
  • Supported compatibility with Intel 12th processor.
  • Added compatibility with Ubuntu 22.04 and kernel 6.1.
  • Optimized compatibility with some third-party cameras.
  • Optimized compatibility with older versions of frame grabber firmwares.
  • Fixed bugs from previous versions.

Reorganized:

  • Reorganized Programming Guide, displaying API calling flows by different function modules, and added some sample codes.

V1.1.0 2022/10

Newly Added:

  • Provided an API for getting SDK version: MV_FG_GetSDKVersion()
  • Provided frame grabber APIs: MV_FG_UpdateInterfaceList() , MV_FG_ReleaseTLayerResource() , MV_FG_GetNumInterfaces() , MV_FG_GetInterfaceInfo() , MV_FG_OpenInterface() , MV_FG_OpenInterfaceEx() , MV_FG_OpenInterfaceByID() , MV_FG_CloseInterface()
  • Provided camera APIs: MV_FG_UpdateDeviceList() , MV_FG_GetNumDevices() , MV_FG_GetDeviceInfo() , MV_FG_OpenDevice() , MV_FG_OpenDeviceByID() , MV_FG_CloseDevice()
  • Provided stream channel APIs: MV_FG_GetNumStreams() , MV_FG_GetPayloadSize() , MV_FG_OpenStream() , MV_FG_CloseStream() , MV_FG_StartAcquisition() , MV_FG_StopAcquisition()
  • Provided APIs for managing buffer: MV_FG_SetBufferNum() , MV_FG_RegisterFrameCallBack() , MV_FG_GetFrameBuffer() , MV_FG_ReleaseFrameBuffer() , MV_FG_GetBufferChunkData() , MV_FG_AnnounceBuffer() , MV_FG_RevokeBuffer() , MV_FG_FlushQueue() , MV_FG_GetImageBuffer() , MV_FG_GetBufferInfo() , MV_FG_QueueBuffer()
  • Provided APIs for image processing: MV_FG_DisplayOneFrame() , MV_FG_SaveBitmap() , MV_FG_SaveJpeg() , MV_FG_SaveTiffToFile() , MV_FG_SavePngToFile() , MV_FG_ConvertPixelType() , MV_FG_ReconstructImage()
  • Provided parameter control APIs: MV_FG_GetXMLFile() , MV_FG_GetNodeAccessMode() , MV_FG_GetNodeInterfaceType() , MV_FG_GetIntValue() , MV_FG_SetIntValue() , MV_FG_GetEnumValue() , MV_FG_SetEnumValue() , MV_FG_SetEnumValueByString() , MV_FG_GetFloatValue() , MV_FG_SetFloatValue() , MV_FG_GetBoolValue() , MV_FG_SetBoolValue() , MV_FG_GetStringValue() , MV_FG_SetStringValue() , MV_FG_SetCommandValue() , MV_FG_FeatureSave() , MV_FG_FeatureLoad()
